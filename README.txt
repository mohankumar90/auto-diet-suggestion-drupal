ABOUT PROJECT
------------

Project aims to automate the process of suggesting diet plans to the patients
based on their health reports & requirements. This is the developed using
Drupal 7.
Drupal is an open source content management platform supporting a variety of
websites ranging from personal weblogs to large community-driven websites.
